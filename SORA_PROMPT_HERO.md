# Sora Prompt for Hero Landing Page Image

```

A sleek, minimalist scene featuring a happy diverse family standing next to a modern car (sedan or SUV) in a bright, clean environment. The car should be prominently displayed on the left side, with the family (2-3 people) on the right, all smiling and looking confident. Soft, professional lighting with a teal and green color palette (#005e54, #007a6e) subtly integrated in the background gradient. The scene should convey trust, savings, and financial security. Clean, modern aesthetic with shallow depth of field, professional photography style. The image should have space on the left or center for text overlay. High quality, 4K resolution, cinematic composition.
```
